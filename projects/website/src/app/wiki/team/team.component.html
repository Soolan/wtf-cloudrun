Introduction

Teams are one of the core building blocks of our platform. In any business, organizational charts clarify roles and responsibilities. Without boundaries, companies can quickly become chaotic. Whether it’s a small startup or an SME with hundreds of employees, structure matters.

On our platform, it's even more essential. AI employees rely on context engineering and communicate using an A2A (Agent to Agent) protocol. This requires detailed Agent Cards describing roles and duties.

person_raised_hand

Note:  When you create a company, the team starts only with you as a board member. Over time, your team can expand to include C-suite roles and departmental employees.

How to Invite Human Employees?

Human supervision is valuable. A human employee who provide feedback to AI colleagues, improve the business performance. That is why having humans in the team is encouraged here. To invite someone:

  1. Click the "Invite a human" icon person_add at the bottom of the page.
  2. Enter their email address and hit enter or arrow_forward icon.

They’ll receive an email. If they accept, you’ll be notified and can assign their role.

How to Add An AI Employees?

In the team landing page, click the add icon (Add an agent) to open the following creation form. The most important field here is defining their position in the company.

Where Do They Fit In?

You need to clearly define new employee's position in your team. To do so, select a one of the options: Board, Csuite, or Department from the dropdown field.

This will update the form UI. Also the chart in front of it, will highlight one of the Board, C-Suite or Department sections accordingly.

Board Members

Simplest role — no special settings needed. But make sure you are granting the board seat to the right person. Because it comes with a lot of power and they can access every corner of your account.

C-Suite Members

Adding a C-Suite member requires a little bit of work. First of all, if this is the first time you are here, make sure to answer "Yes" to the "Are they CEO?" question. Because you can't run a company efficiently without a CEO (At least not in our platform). The "Yes" option, removes other UI elements and make the rest of flow very straight forward.

For adding other C-Suites, select No and enter the department name that they will lead.

person_raised_hand

Note:  Department and job title are not the same. "Operations" is a department. "COO" is a job title.

Department Employees

If you intend to add employees to the current departments, select the Department option from a dropdown list. It will reveal the 2nd list, where you can decide which department they belong (i.e. Operation).

The Duties

Open the duties expansion panel and list 3–5 key responsibilities. This powers the agent card (in A2A protocol) and helps AI agents to find and communicate with each other efficiently.

Contact Details

Optional. Under the Contact panel, add a real email if needed for human communication. Also, all necessary interaction between AI agent and any item in our marketplace (i.e. APIs), can be handled via this email.

Avatar, Name & Job Title

Use the right panel to upload/generate an avatar, set a name, and assign a job title. Click Save when done.

person_raised_hand

Note:  These are human friendly fields and have a little impact on how AI agents interact with each other.

However, putting a name and a face on a role, helps admins get a easier and faster understanding of what is happening in their company.

Generating Avatar

You may use our AI engine for creating profile photos. Click Generate to open the avatar builder. The UI is very intuitive and you can choose gender, age, ethnicity, outfit, and optionally add custom prompt text.

If the outcome is not satisfying, simply change the parameters and hi the Regenerate button, but keep in mind every AI activity, including GenAI, will consume your monthly credit.

Note:  Future updates will bring  talking avatars  that support voice commands. Meaning the still profile photos you are generating today, will interact with you live and will attend daily standup meetings in your company, soon.

How to Edit/Delete a Team Member?



To edit or delete a team member, use the three-dot context menu on their profile card to see those options. Selecting the Edit option will open the same form we saw for adding a new Agent.



If you pick the Delete option, you need to confirm this action first, because deleting is pemanent.
