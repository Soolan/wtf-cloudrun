Introduction

When you register on our platform, your onboarding flow begins by automatically creating:

  • A new company
  • A team, with you as a board member and admin
  • A Kanban board for task management
  • A blank playbook

You’ll also receive a Pro trial valid for one month, which includes:

  • 50GB of storage
  • 200,000 AI tokens
person_raised_hand

Note:  You can compare our subscription plans and their features arrow_forward here

After registration, you’ll be redirected to the console area, where the dashboard displays all companies created under your account.

View and Edit a Company

Click the View button inside a company card to access its landing page, where you can see and update its details.

New companies will have:

  • A default name and description
  • Empty branding (no logo or banner)

Managing Logo and Banner

If you already have branding assets:

  1. Click the Browse button under the logo or banner section.
  2. Upload your file.
  3. Use the preview window to crop the image as needed.
person_raised_hand

Note:  Logos are forced to have  1:1  aspect ratio, and banners require  12:3  (wide) aspect ratio. So, for best fit, follow the provided aspect ratios.

To edit or delete an asset, hover over the logo or banner area — delete delete and edit edit icons will appear.

person_raised_hand

Note:  Uploaded or generated assets are stored in your account. Deleting files will free up space. You can track these actions in the Statement tab under your Subscription.

Generating a Logo

If you don’t have a logo, you can generate one using the platform. Click Generate and customize the following:

  • Style: Minimal, Modern, Abstract, Logotype, Monogram, Emblem, Vintage, Pictorial, Combination
  • Background: Minimal, Modern, Abstract, Solid, Gradient, Sketch, Patterned, Nature, Photo
  • Business: Enter your industry or business type

Changes are reflected in the prompt box below. You can also add a custom prompt (max 100 characters). Use the delete icon to remove any custom chunks if needed.

Generating a Banner

The banner generator works similarly to the logo generator. You can:

  • Choose a style
  • Define your business/industry
  • Add custom sentences (up to 100 characters)

Once ready, hit the generate button and banner will be created automatically.

Editing Company Name and Description

Below the banner, you’ll see the default company name and description.

Hover to reveal the Edit (pencil) icon. Click to edit inline, and press Enter (or Return on Mac), or click the checkmark icon to save your changes.

The Report Section

This section provides insights into your company’s activity and usage.

Activity (Left Panel)

  • Tickets created, assigned, or delivered
  • New employees (AI or human) added
  • Changes to the Playbook
  • Marketplace interactions (install/uninstall packages)

Usage (Right Panel)

Doughnut charts show:

  • Storage usage (in GB)
  • AI token usage for AI-related activities

Availability depends on your subscription tier.

Add a New Company

Company limits based on subscription:

  • Free: 1 company
  • Basic: Up to 2 companies
  • Pro: Up to 3 companies

To add a new company:

  1. Use the side navigation menu.
  2. Click the top dropdown and select + Add Company.

This will launch a dialog guiding you through creating a new blank company, team, Kanban board, and playbook — just like the original registration flow.

After creation, you can edit the company details as described above.

Delete, Backup, and Restore Company Data

To manage your company data:

  1. Click the three-dot menu next to the company.
  2. Select an option:
    • Delete the company
    • Backup its data
    • Restore from a backup

Deleting a company

It is critical to understand, upon deleting a company, its data will be removed completely to release space for new creations. Also, you get a chance to backup the company first, in case you changed your mind later.

Backup and restore

When backing up a company, you get to chose which databases (collections) you like to keep.

The backup file will be stored in your account automatically. You may also download them if you prefer to keep it as a local file.

person_raised_hand

Note:  Downloading backups is essential if you intend to create a new company, (in your account or somewhere else) and clone your previous company there.

During the restore, (if the company backup file existed) you may click on it and follow the instructions. However if this is a new company, you can upload backup files and let the platform extract and restore it for you.
