Problem

Look at the "Assets" column on your balance sheet. Do you see your talent capital listed there? No? That’s the problem!

Talent is hard to quantify, which is why it’s often missing from the balance sheet. While businesses race to automate, scale, and streamline, a fundamental asset remains undervalued and unmanaged: talent itself.

Teams grow, bloat, and churn. But unlike software, talent lacks an infrastructure layer—it can’t be versioned, scaled, or monetized the same way.

Talent is only as valuable as its continued presence. And when someone walks out the door, the time and money you invested in them walks out too. Meaning, you'll write off the wasted resources as an expense.

That’s not scale. That’s leakage.
